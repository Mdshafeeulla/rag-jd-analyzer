Job Title: Data Analyst

We are looking for a Data Analyst to join our team.

REQUIREMENTS
- 1-3 years of experience in data analysis
- Proficient in Python (pandas, numpy)
- Strong SQL skills for querying databases
- Experience with data visualization (Power BI or Tableau)
- Ability to communicate insights to non-technical stakeholders

RESPONSIBILITIES
- Build and maintain automated data pipelines
- Create dashboards and reports for business teams
- Perform exploratory data analysis on large datasets
- Collaborate with product and engineering teams
